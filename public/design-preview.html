The Big Picture

Two apps, two worlds

DWDC is your brand — polished, outward-facing, professional. FIG is your brain — personal, internal, how you actually think and work. They should never look the same.

DWDC
Your business. Outward-facing.
Forest Green Blush Pink Serif Type Performance Energy
FIG
Your brain. Inward-facing.
Warm Charcoal Amber Gold Grounded Type Thinker Energy
My Pick

A + B Hybrid — "Warm Ink" bones, "Soft Stone" warmth

Use Concept A's charcoal base and amber accents for dark mode (your default). But bring in Concept B's cream warmth and Fraunces font for light mode — giving you two genuinely different feeling modes. Both feel personal. Neither feels like DWDC. Terracotta as a secondary accent bridges both modes.

If You Say Go

What actually changes

This isn't a rebuild. It's a reskin. Same functionality, same data, same architecture. Just different colors, fonts, and spacing.

1. New default theme
Replace the Forest theme with the chosen palette as default. Update CSS custom properties.
2. Typography upgrade
Load 1-2 Google Fonts. Apply serif for headlines, sans-serif for body. Self-host for PWA offline.
3. Refresh all 6 theme options
Update the theme switcher palettes so none overlap with DWDC's forest/blush identity.
4. Spacing & radius pass
Slightly larger card radius, more generous padding. Premium feel without layout changes.
5. New app icon
Regenerate PWA icons in the new primary color. Amber FIG on charcoal, or terracotta on cream.
